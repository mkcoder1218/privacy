1. Introduction

Red Sea market ("we", "our", or "us") is a B2C marketplace mobile application that connects businesses with consumers for the sale and purchase of products and services. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

By using Red Sea market, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information

We may collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Delivery and billing address
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Identity verification documents (only if required)

b) Usage Data

  • App activity and feature usage
  • Device information (e.g., device model, OS version, IP address)
  • Crash logs and diagnostics

c) Location Information

With your permission, we may access your location to enable features such as location-based listings, delivery tracking, and fraud prevention.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Facilitate transactions between buyers and sellers
  • Process orders and manage deliveries
  • Provide customer support
  • Send notifications related to your orders, account, or promotions
  • Improve our app performance and features
  • Ensure compliance with applicable laws

4. Sharing of Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share it in the following cases:

  • With sellers: To fulfill your orders
  • With third-party service providers: For payment processing, hosting, analytics, and delivery services

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to provide services and meet legal obligations. You may request to delete your data or account by contacting us.
